Algorithm Guide
FIFO Simple

Evicts the oldest page in memory. Predictable but prone to Belady's Anomaly.

LRU Practical

Evicts the least recently used page. Works well in real workloads; widely implemented.

Optimal Theoretical

Evicts the page farthest in the future. Minimum faults — only a benchmark, needs future knowledge.
